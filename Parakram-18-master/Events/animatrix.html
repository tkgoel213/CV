“Let’s shape our imagination"

The event is for all those folks who’re into Film Making and video editing. The event is all about creativity and shaping your imagination through videos.

Problem Statement ::

  • A scene based on a theme will be provided which is to be implemented with the help of any video editing software, Adobe After Effects preferably. Using the CHROMA KEY effect/ MASKING mainly the team have submit their animated videos.

Rounds and details ::

  • The event will be of three days from the starting day of Parakram. A video booth with a green background, will be displayed for the teams to record the clip scenes
  • Teams have to shoot the clips ,compile them, edit them, voice over : in short do every possible editing to make the video more likely
  • The background images/videos over which the clips have to masked will be revealed on the first day of the fest
  • The edited video has to be send via mail or handed in person the the Event coordinator.

Judging Criteria :

  • The perfection in shooting the clips.
  • The editing of the videos.
  • Last but not the least Team spirit! . Make sure every team member has a descent contribution in it.
