“We want Google to become the third half of your brain” -- Sergey Brin

Parakram-2k18 brings to you Googler, a challenge designed to test your art of Gooooooooogling.

Problem Statement ::

  • You will be given a piece of information in the form of textual or visual content. Your objective is to generate a Google search result based on the given information within the specified time constraint.

Rounds and details ::

  • You can form a team of exactly two candidates, and any number of teams can participate.
  • 7 teams will be shortlisted for the final round based on the scores of the shortlisting round.
  • All the rounds will be consisting of a set of problems associated with a specified time limit.
  • Your task is to generate an exact Google search result based on the problem / information.
  • The team which first reports the exact Google search result will be rewarded with points

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ::

·         Q1: Can I particpate as an individual candidate?

          Ans: No, you cannot participate as an individual candidate.

·         Q2: Do I have to arrange for laptop?

          Ans: Yes, you are required to come with a laptop and an internet connection.
