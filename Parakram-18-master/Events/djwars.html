"EAT-SLEEP-MIX-REPEAT" - Every DJ Ever

Being amongst the few of remix competition at college level in India, DJ wars is here to spread the DJing culture and motivate the upcoming and aspiring DJs to pursue music.



Problem Statement :

  • Participants will be provided with the Acapella of a song and they will have to build music around the Acapella, thus creating their own version of the song, ie, the remix of the song.

Rounds and details :

  • Participants will be given a time limit of 10 days from the day Acapella is uploaded on the website.
  • Use of any DAW is allowed.
  • Participants will have to send their remix on the email provided before the deadline.

Judging Criteria :

  • Judging will be done on the basis of how cachy the remix is and the creativity of the DJ.
